About Us

Committed to Your Health and Well-being

Dakha Devi Memorial Hospital is a premier multi-speciality hospital in Chomu, Jaipur, offering expert medical care in cardiology, respiratory diseases, women’s health, and dental care.

Our facility is run by a team of highly experienced doctors who are also associated with leading hospitals in Jaipur. We are dedicated to bringing advanced medical expertise, modern diagnostic technology, and compassionate care closer to local patients in rural and urban areas.
